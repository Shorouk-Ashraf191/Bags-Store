About Tote Bag

What is a Tote Bag

25th May 2020

A tote bag is an incredibly useful accessory. It’s a simple product with huge versatility. In the most basic terms, a tote bag is defined as a mid-large sized bag which has two parallel handles. Tote bags are usually unfastened at the top and are bigger than most other handbags.

Tote bags can be made from a range of different fabrics and come in multiple colours and patterns. They are often made from lightweight materials such as canvas and cotton or from woven fabrics like jute. However, Tote bags can also be made from more durable fabrics such as leather.

History of the Tote Bag

5th june 2022

The American word tote or toting has uncertain origins. But many believe that it originated from West Africa. In Kikongo, the word ‘tota’ means “to pick up.” And in Swahili, ‘tuta’ means “to carry.” But when did it become more than just a word?

In 1944, the outdoor brand L. L.Bean introduced its famous Ice Bag. Its purpose was to help carry ice from your car to your freezer, hence the name. But the bag’s popularity went beyond ice-carrying. Bean took note of this and dressed up the bag with different color and design options.

How to Choose the Correct Size For You

30th september 2022

So what size tote back is right for you? Large tote bags are for those who don’t have time to go home for an outfit change between work and the gym. Or for people who need to have their laptop on hand at all times. If that sounds like you, you’re probably in the market for a large carryall tote bag. A medium tote bag is for someone who wants a bag that can easily go from daywear to nightwear without skipping a beat.

The small bag is for those who like a tote bag’s look, but prefer a more manageable size. This bag would just carry the essentials like your wallet, keys, and lipstick.
